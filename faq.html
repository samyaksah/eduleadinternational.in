Questions

Find answers before you enquire.

Common questions about programme choice, online options, certification, cohorts, and school training.

Which programme should I choose?

Teaching and Learning is suited for classroom practice. Educational Leadership is suited for coordinators, heads, principals, and aspiring leaders.

Are programmes available online?

CPD Online is structured for active online learning, and other delivery details can be discussed based on cohort needs.

Can schools request customized training?

Yes. Certified training programmes can be adapted to a school's goals, staff needs, and development priorities.

Do participants receive certification?

Certification is included at the end of certified training programmes and applicable course pathways.

How do I ask about a cohort?

Use the enquiry form or contact EduLead International directly by phone or email.
