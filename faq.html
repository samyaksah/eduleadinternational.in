General FAQs

Programmes and admissions.

1. What programmes does Edu Lead International offer?

Edu Lead International offers:

  • Cambridge International Certificate/Diploma in Teaching and Learning (CIDTL)
  • Cambridge International Certificate/Diploma in Educational Leadership (CIDEL)
  • School-based CPD programmes
  • Customised teacher and leadership training
  • Professional development workshops and webinars
2. Are your programmes recognised internationally?

Yes. The Cambridge International Certificate and Diploma programmes are internationally recognised professional development qualifications.

3. Can international participants enrol in the programme?

Yes. Educators from any country are welcome to apply, provided they meet the programme requirements.

4. Is there an age limit for enrolment?

No. There is no age restriction for participation.

5. What level of English proficiency is required?

Participants should be comfortable reading academic texts, participating in discussions, and producing written assignments in English.

6. How do I know which programme is right for me?

Choose Teaching and Learning if your primary focus is improving classroom practice and student outcomes. Choose Educational Leadership if your focus is leading teams, driving school improvement, and developing organisational capacity.

7. Can I speak to an advisor before applying?

Absolutely. Our programme advisors can help you determine which qualification best aligns with your professional goals and experience. You can use the enquiry form or call/WhatsApp us at +91 8800542932.

Cambridge CIDTL FAQs

Teaching and Learning.

8. Who can enrol in the Cambridge International Certificate/Diploma in Teaching and Learning?

The programme is designed for practising teachers who wish to enhance their classroom practice and improve student learning outcomes.

9. How long does the programme take to complete?

The Certificate Programme takes 6 months, while the Diploma Programme takes 8 months to complete.

10. Is this programme conducted online or offline?

It is delivered completely online, enabling educators from across India and abroad to participate conveniently.

11. What are the benefits of completing CIDTL?

Participants develop:

  • Research-informed teaching practices
  • Reflective teaching skills
  • Assessment literacy
  • Student-centred learning strategies
  • Evidence-based classroom improvement skills
12. Do I need prior Cambridge teaching experience?

No. Teachers from CBSE, ICSE, State Boards, IB, Cambridge, and other educational systems can apply.

13. Can I pay the fee in instalments?

Yes, an instalment option is available. Please check the Teaching and Learning fee structure.

14. When does the next cohort begin?

The next Teaching and Learning cohort begins on July 24, 2026.

Programme Delivery FAQs

Sessions, assignments, and certification.

22. How are the sessions conducted?

One session of 1.5 hours per week is conducted online. Sessions are highly interactive and involve discussions, case studies, collaborative learning, reflection, and practical application tasks.

23. Who is the Programme Leader?

Ms. Rupam Sah is the accredited Programme Leader for the Cambridge International Diploma in Teaching and Learning (CIDTL) and the Cambridge International Diploma in Educational Leadership (CIDEL). With extensive experience in educator development and school leadership, she provides expert facilitation, mentoring, and ongoing support to help participants successfully achieve their professional learning goals.

24. How much time should I dedicate each week?

Participants should typically allocate 3-5 hours per week for reading, reflection, assignments, and portfolio development.

25. What kind of assignments are involved?

Assignments generally require participants to apply learning in their own educational context and provide evidence of impact on teaching, learning, or leadership.

26. Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

Yes, successful participants receive the relevant qualification certificate awarded by Cambridge International Education.
