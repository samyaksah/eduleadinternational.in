About the Course

Online professional development for active classrooms.

This programme is designed to empower aspiring and in-service teachers, school academic heads, and school administrators towards creating an active learning environment inside classrooms.

The course is focused on pedagogical skills that are significant for the needs of 21st-century learners. Learners also get exposure to technological tools that can be embedded in lesson plans to create interesting and enthusing learning environments.

15 online interactive sessions spread over 4 months.
2-hour sessions conducted once per week.
Personalised learning environment for educators.
Exposure to technology tools for lesson planning.

Programme Topics

Focused sessions for classroom-ready practice.

Active learning online sessions are conducted on the following topics.

01

Active Learning Strategies

Explore ways to make learners more engaged, participative, and reflective during lessons.

02

Differentiated Instruction

Design instruction that responds to varied learner needs, readiness, and learning styles.

03

Metacognitive Strategies

Build reflective practice and use metacognition strategies effectively in the classroom.

04

Meaningful Assessments

Create assessment practices that support learning and provide useful feedback for students.

05

Relationship Management

Strengthen classroom relationships, communication, and positive learning environments.

06

Blended Learning

Use synchronous and asynchronous learning environments to engage students effectively.

Teacher Skills

A comprehensive 30-hour training programme.

CPD Online guides teachers with the skills needed to design innovative strategies, plan active lessons, integrate differentiated instruction, use questioning techniques, and employ technology effectively.

The programme also helps teachers become reflective practitioners and carve out a better version of themselves every passing day.

Design their own innovative strategies.
Plan every minute of the class and create active lesson plans.
Integrate differentiated instruction for a more inclusive class.
Create a questioning classroom and use correct questioning techniques.
Use a variety of assessments to check students' learning.
Employ technology to engage students in synchronous and asynchronous learning environments.
Build a firm hold on metacognition and classroom strategies to use it effectively.
Become reflective practitioners through continuous professional growth.

Assessment and Certification

Assessment that supports classroom growth.

The CPD Online Programme evaluates professional knowledge, instructional practice, lesson planning, assessment literacy, classroom implementation, and reflective growth.

50%Written assignments
30%Lesson observation and practical application
10%Participation and engagement
10%Reflection and professional growth portfolio

Assignment Focus

Teaching strategies, lesson planning, assessment literacy, blended and flipped learning, and summative application tasks.

Observation Focus

Learning environment, student motivation, lesson structure, questioning, differentiation, assessment practices, classroom management, communication, and professionalism.

Completion Requirements

Participants complete required assignments, participate actively, submit tasks on time, achieve at least 60%, and demonstrate satisfactory professional practice.

The assessment philosophy emphasizes growth, reflection, and practical classroom application, so teachers are supported in developing practices that positively impact student learning outcomes.
